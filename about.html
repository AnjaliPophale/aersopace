JK Maini Aerospace is a trusted name in precision engineering, delivering mission-critical components and solutions for the global aerospace industry.

About JK Maini Global Aerospace Limited

Founded in 1973, the Maini Group is a diversified $200 million global conglomerate, renowned for its leadership in Material Handling, Precision Manufacturing, and E-Mobility Solutions.

In a strategic merger with the prestigious Raymond Group, the legacy of engineering excellence now evolves into a stronger, unified identity—JK Maini Precision Parts. As the dedicated aerospace division, JK Maini Global Aerospace Limited (JKMGAL) continues to build on this legacy, delivering world-class aerospace components, complex assemblies, and precision-engineered systems for global aviation leaders.

With a future-ready mindset, cutting-edge infrastructure, and a focus on sustainability, JKMGAL is redefining aerospace manufacturing for a new era of innovation and growth.

Future-Ready 25-Acre Aerospace Facility

Our new 25-acre state-of-the-art facility in Hindupur, Andhra Pradesh, is purpose-built to meet the dynamic needs of the global aerospace industry. Designed with sustainability and scalability in mind, the facility reflects our commitment to long-term innovation and environmental stewardship.

  • In-house special process capabilities: Heat treatment, Surface coating, NDT, and more
  • Asset-light & partnership-ready model for JVs and strategic collaborations
  • Paris Agreement 2050-aligned sustainability goals
  • Fully integrated renewable energy systems
  • Low-carbon footprint operations to support net-zero manufacturing

Expanding Global Manufacturing Footprint
